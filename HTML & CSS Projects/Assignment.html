Curse of Strahd

A D&D adventure where travelers find themselves trapped in the dark and mysterious land of Barovia, fief to the curious leader, Count Strahd von Zarovich.

Count Strahd von Zarovich was a vampire and the dark lord of the valley of Barovia, one of the Domains of Dread located in a remote corner of the Shadowfell.
Players must navigate this treacherous land and Strahd's machinations if they ever wish to return home.

The Bone Grinder

A scenario our travelers must face as they make their way to the city of Vallaki. An innocent seeming trio of elderly woman, and a dark secret they keep in their windmill.

Death Scenario: Rudolph

In the case our travelers find themselves on the bad end of a hostile encounter during the Bone Grinder scenario there is an alternative to death. Our nasty trio has a use for travelers such as these. To accept would spare their lives, though at a steep cost.
